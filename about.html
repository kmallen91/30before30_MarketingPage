About the App

We got together as a group to create this application because we felt that we could provide a tool for people to help organize how they plan and accomplish their bucket lists. We wanted to create an intuitive environment where anyone can brainstorm their bucket list and then keep track of the progress they make, no matter how long it takes to accomplish!
